Taniti Resturants.

Currently, we have 10 total resturants. Some serving American-style meals, and some serve Pan-asian Cuisine, but most serve local fish and rice.

Exactly half (5) of our local resturants serve meals consisting of mostly local fish and rice.

Three of our resturants serve American-style meals. Just in case you need a taste of home!

Two of our eating establishments here on Taniti serve mostly Pan-Asian cuisine.

If you're the type that likes to cook, we have two supermarkets, two smaller grocery stores, and one 24 hour convenience store. All of which can provide you with the local flavor essentials you'll need to capture the spirit of Taniti in every dish.
