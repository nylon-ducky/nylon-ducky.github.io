Activities

Taniti has plenty of entertainment activities for visitors young and old. For the youngsters and the fun-loving adults alike, we have an arcade where you can play all the favorites from Skee-Ball to Pac-Man

The island also has a bowling alley, providing fun for the whole family!

There's a new dance club too for all you party animals out there.

If you're in the mood for something more laid-back, theres a movie theater on the island as well as a local history museum and some art galleries.

The island has several pubs and even a local microbrewery for everyone who can appreciate a good beverage.
