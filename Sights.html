Sightseeing

Just one of the sightseeing activities we offer is a thrilling Helicopter tour of the island.

Many of our visitors enjoy exploring the jungle which is free and easy to do, either by foot or bike rental.

Some of the most bold and daring adventurers even go so far as to hike to our volcano!

If you're into fishing, you can even go on a chartered fishing tour with one of our many local fishermen and cook what you catch.

Maybe you're more interested in getting a close up look at some under the sea wildlife here in Taniti. Snorkeling just might be the perfect activity for you.

At the end of the day, you can always sit back and relax at one of our beautiful, sandy beaches.
